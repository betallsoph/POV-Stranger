# Privacy Policy — POV-Stranger

**Last updated:** 2026-07-14  
**Contact:** [your support email]

## What we collect

- **Apple Sign In** anonymous identifier (no name or email stored in-app)
- **Approximate location:** country code and timezone only — never precise GPS
- **Photos** you choose to share during an active 24-hour session
- **Device push token** for session and photo notifications
- **Farewell message** (max 280 characters, deleted after session purge)

## What we do not collect

- Real name, email, phone number, or social profiles
- Precise location coordinates
- Contacts or address book
- Analytics advertising identifiers (MVP)

## How data is used

- Match you with one anonymous stranger per session
- Relay hourly photos between matched users
- Deliver push notifications for photos and session lifecycle
- Enforce safety (reports, blocks, content moderation)

## Retention

- Session photos and messages: **deleted within ~25 hours** (server TTL + purge job)
- Block list: retained to prevent rematching blocked users
- Pair history: anonymized metadata only (no photos), kept for weekly rematch rules

## Your rights

You may request deletion of your account data by contacting support. Sign in with Apple allows revoking access from Apple ID settings.

## Children

POV-Stranger is **17+** only. We do not knowingly collect data from users under 17.
